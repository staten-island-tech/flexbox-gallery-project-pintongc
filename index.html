Unique Animals

resources

Stick Insect

One of the best natural camouflages on Earth, closely mimicing the appearance of a stick. Able to stay still for a very long time.

Glass Frog

Frog with transparent skin. From the underside, their organs are visible. They also have spotted backs which resemble eggs.

Wombat

Fuzzy creatures with unique features. Their pouches face backwards and they poop in cubes.

Axolotl

A salamander with legs that never reach land but roam on the ocean floor. Also a mob in Minecraft.

Lyrebird

Exceptional ability in mimicing sounds found within their environment, this includes just about anything, like power tools.

Aye-Aye

Nocturnal lemurs with big ears and rat teeth. They use sound feedback to locate insects in trees after tapping on the tree.

Okapi

Only living relative of the giraffe but has black and white marks like a zebra.

Markhor

Goat with horns shaped like corkscrews, they also possess a long beard. Used to be endangered but with the help of conservation efforts, is no longer on the endangered list.

Mexican Mole Lizard

Looks like a worm but is actually a lizard. Stubby legs with 5 fingers and dots for eyes.

Poodle Moth

Covered with hair which makes it look like a poodle. Loves light just like a normal moth though.

Leaf-Tailed Gecko

Has a tail that looks like a dried out, brown leaf. Even has veins that replicate those on a leaf.

Panda Ant

Fuzzy, black and white, just like a panda. Not as innocent though as they can become dangerous with their stings.
